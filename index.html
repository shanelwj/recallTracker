recallTracker — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

No data collected

recallTracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The product label information you enter (brand, model, lot number) is used only to search for recalls on your device and is never sent anywhere.

On-device matching

All recall matching runs locally using CPSC recall data bundled inside the app. No network requests are made. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.
